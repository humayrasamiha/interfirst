LOAN PROGRAMS

WHOLESALE - LOAN PROGRAMS

We provide our brokers with a comprehensive mix of conforming and non-conforming loan products that serve conventional borrowers.

Conventional Conforming Loans

  • Loan amounts to $417,000 for single family residence.
  • Fixed-rate: 10, 15, 20, 25 and 30 year terms available
  • VAdjustable-rate: 5/1 and 7/1 terms available
  • Down payment as low as 5% available
  • Lender paid Mortgage Insurance available
  • Primary residence,homes and investment properties

Conforming Jumbo/High Balance Loans

  • Loans $417,000 to $625,500 for family residence.
  • Fixed-rate: 10, 15, 20, 25 and 30 year terms available
  • Adjustable-rate: 5/1 and 7/1 terms available
  • Down payment as low as 5% available
  • Lender paid Mortgage Insurance available
  • Primary residence,homes and investment properties

Non-Conforming Jumbo Loans

  • Loan amounts up to $2 million
  • Fixed-rate: 15 and 30 year terms available
  • Adjustable-rate: 5/1 and 7/1 terms available
  • Minimum 20% down payment required
  • Primary residence and second homes only
  • Adjustable-rate: 5/1 and 7/1 terms available
